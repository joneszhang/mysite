Gem Optimization(2013.9.10 - 2013.12.25)

This is for the curriculum design of the course Computer Graphics. In this class we use Opengl to impelment theories we have learnedfrom class. For the 16 weeks of courses, wee needed to add new functions into our existed projects and did a presentation, in the last project our teacher asked us to reach the goals below.

1. Creating three diferent objects. applicate Hierarchical modeling in at least one of the models.
2. Including at least two different kinds of light, one of which can be adjusted.
3. Implement scene roaming.
4. Switch between different viewpoints.
5. Texture mapping

team work

Our teacher asked us to work as part of a team so I gathered together with my two friends as a team. As team leader, I held a short meeting very week to contribute our task. Usually I was responsible for the construction of scene and the implemets the roaming function and they did works like modeling and keyboard input response. Every week after normal class we will gater together to discuss about the theories our teacher had taught and design the way we implemented it. Usually about 3 dyas later we will finish our works respectlively and then they would give me their codes and explained them to me. Finally I would gather all codes together and made some modification. Then we could made representation at next Monday.

What's special

Besides the fundamental demands from our teacher, I've also implemented some very interesting function by myself. First is add gravity and so that makes the roaming more realistic. To do this I add a fixed accelerated speed every time when calculating the little bear's speed so that when it jumps, the effect of acceleration and deceleration will make its motion more realistic. Also, after optimizing the keyboard response, the motion of the little bear became more smooth especially when it turns direction.

Another funny function, as you can see in the video, is a huge ball that gradually drops down and largens. It use the sphere manner of texure, which makes the portraits of Chairman Mao floating on the ball and could move with the motion of your vision.

Besides, I also implemented the boundary detection. Via monitoring the coordinate of the little bear constantly and compare it with the boundary of other objects I can decide wheter it now collides with other objects. If so, I will correct it's coordinate into the normal value. Because the correct range is small, it will looks like the bear is bounced back, which is very ideal.
