Intelligent insole

Via insert a microcontroller inside an insole, we get a new wearable device. Looks like it's quiet simple, but quiet large amount of work has been done. First we did a lot of research before deciding where to put the microcontroller and finally decided to insert it in the center of insole so that when people wear shoes it will be under the foot arch and the archy room will help protect the microcontroller from being grinded to broken. Second, the communication between microcontroller and mobile phone was another difficulty. We chose bluetooth to solve this problem and the bluetooth development based on microcontroller was really difficult. We carefully researched the Bluetooth protocol and wrote a whole module for bluetooth communication. And finally, we build an android App for users to control the insole and help manage their health condition. We also build a website for the promotion of our products.

Frenquency of operation

To solve the problem of bandwith and unstability. I limit the frequecny of click remote-control into 2 times a second. which will give the machine some time to do the transportation work. To finish this I use time.h to read the time of the last click and compare with the next click, if the time is within 0.5 seconds, I will neglect it and record it as the last click.

For some extreme condition in which the machine may dump irregularlly, I took a manner of necessity. For a group of drawing action, I seperately finish them every, say, 10 milliseconds, which may diminish the experience, but will guarantee the stability cause 10 miliseconds is enough for any computer to transport dozens kb of data.
