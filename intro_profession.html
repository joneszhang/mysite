Profession

I'm now an undergraduate of School of Software, Southeast University, major in Software Engineering. Our school offers us 39 specialized courses involved in aspects including computer science, engineering, programming, mathematics, physics and so on. The systematical plan of our school have offered me thoroughly understanding of computer structure from the constitution of cpu to object-oriented programming. After about 3 years of learning I've absorbed abundant knowledge and understood the outline of CS and Software Engineering and ready for my graduate study. Besides, as the member of English teaching program, the tuition and curriculum design are all finished in English, which has prepared me well for studying in America.

Software Engineering

As the major of my undergraduate study, our school has offered us a systematic plan for understanding how a market-oriented product was created. Throughout the three years we have learned classes like Introduction to Software Engineering, Software Testing, Database Principle, Software Architecture and so on, which have covered all steps of software production. Besides knowledge, all courses have very interesting curriculum design and with which we have absorbed practical methods of solving problems in various aspects of software engineering.

Programming

With strong background of engineering, our school never neglects the development of our practical ability. During our undergraduate life our school has invited companies like Oracle to organize practical training for us twice, during which we work in team and produced software in very professional way. Besides, due to the enthusiasm for CS, I've participated many programs myself and have already worked with dozens of programming language like C#, C++, SQL, JAVA, HTML, PHP and so on, which has given me special understanding of programming language and experience of research and engineering.

Computer Science

We have also systematically learned about computer science. Our school asked us to take dozens of courses like Operator System, Input&Output System, Principle of Computer, Data structure, Principle of Compile. During study we have experiences things like construct digital circuits, compile linux core, which have given us specific knowledge of computer sturcture and the principle it obeys and have laid strong fundation for future study of Computer Science.
